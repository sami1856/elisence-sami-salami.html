About Sami

Sami Salami is the founder and architect of the global digital health ecosystem Elisence. He works on advanced health intelligence, sovereign AI architecture, and non-diagnostic safety systems for families, clinicians, ministries, and government-level public health platforms.

Elisence

Elisence is a multi-phase digital health and humanity platform designed across UK, GCC, and global healthcare standards (NHS, GCC PDPL, EU MDR, FDA CDS, Zero-Trust, BBVP methodology).
